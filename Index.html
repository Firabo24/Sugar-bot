What is SUGARBOT?

SUGARBOT is a solar-powered, autonomous AI robot that detects and removes weeds, covers exposed sugarcane roots, and improves yield‚Äîwithout the use of herbicides or manual labor.

  • 60% less manual weeding
  • 20% higher yields
  • Offline functionality
  • Built in Ethiopia, for Ethiopia

The Problem

  • Manual labor is time-consuming and costly
  • Herbicide use damages the environment and health
  • Sugarcane roots often remain exposed
  • Rural youth face limited job opportunities

How It Works

Using AI vision and GPS, the robot autonomously navigates sugarcane fields, identifies weeds, removes them with precision, and covers exposed roots using a soil-blade mechanism. It runs on solar power and sends updates via mobile dashboard.
